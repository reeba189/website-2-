Welcome to Muslim Students Board — Nowgam, Srinagar

We empower students through community programs, academic support and cultural activities. Join us to learn, lead and serve.

MSB Nowgam is dedicated to student welfare, cultural preservation and academic enrichment. We run mentoring sessions, scholarships, and community outreach throughout the year.

  • Provide academic guidance and resources
  • Promote cultural & social activities
  • Support student careers and leadership

Regular events include study camps, cultural nights, seminars and community aid programs involving students and local partners.

About MSB Nowgam

Founded to represent and support the student community of Nowgam and nearby areas, the Muslim Students Board organizes academic help, social services and cultural events while fostering leadership among youth.

Activities

Study Circles

Peer-led study groups and tutoring for board & university exams.

Cultural Nights

Celebrations of local music, poetry and arts.

Community Service

Health drives, literacy camps and local outreach activities.
