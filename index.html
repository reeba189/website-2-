History

Cluster University Srinagar was founded to consolidate academic resources and enhance higher education delivery in the Kashmir valley. Over the years, the university has grown through partnerships with regional colleges, research initiatives and public engagement programs.

CUS blends traditional values and local culture with modern curricula and international best practices to prepare graduates for both local impact and global opportunities.
